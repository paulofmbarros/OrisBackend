## Guidelines

**This document should:**
- ✅ Never change week-to-week
- ✅ Be referenced in every planning session
- ✅ Resolve strategic ambiguity
- ✅ Provide context for AI agents
- ✅ Be readable in <5 minutes

**This document should NOT:**
- ❌ Contain tactical plans (those go in plan.yaml)
- ❌ List features (those go in Jira)
- ❌ Track current status (that's in context.md)
- ❌ Document processes (that's in other docs)

**Anti-patterns:**
- Treating this as a living roadmap (it's not)
- Making it too long (>2 pages is too much)
- Making it too generic (be specific about your product)
- Never reviewing it (review quarterly)
